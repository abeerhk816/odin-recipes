Salad

Description

This fresh garden salad is full of crisp vegetables and tossed with a light vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Toss in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.
  4. Serve fresh.
