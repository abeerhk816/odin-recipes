Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are easy to make and delicious with syrup and fruit on top. A perfect breakfast treat!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add milk and eggs.
  3. Whisk into smooth batter.
  4. Cook on a hot griddle until golden.
