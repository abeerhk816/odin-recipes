Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe is perfect for family dinners...

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
